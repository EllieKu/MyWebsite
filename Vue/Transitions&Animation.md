# Transitions & Animation

## Transition
- 在下列情況中可以給任何元素或組件添加進入/離開過渡：
    - `v-if`
    - `v-show`
    - 動態組件
    - 組建根節點
- Transition Classes(過渡的類名)
    - `v-enter`
    - `v-enter-active`
    - `v-enter-to`
    - `v-leave`
    - `v-leave-active`
    - `v-leave-to`
